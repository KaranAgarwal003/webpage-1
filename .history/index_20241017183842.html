About

The template in this example is inspired by W3Schools' parallax template. The original template has a simplistic and elegant design using a fixed background image and smooth scrolling to create a parallax effect.

The parallax scrolling effect makes background images move slower than the foreground content, creating a sense of depth on a page.

It's perfect for one-page websites that focus on storytelling or portfolios.
